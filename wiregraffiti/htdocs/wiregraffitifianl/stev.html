Stev is the bands secret weapon, a multi-instrumentalist (his drumming is as good as his guitar playing) and sound engineer, he is responsible for recording Wire Graffiti's CDs and doing a large part of the arrangments. Stev also records and produces other bands and has recently finished projects by Beautiful Engines, Clever Girl, Drip Joy, Pfiffin, Vir, Dynamite 8, Dolorata and Agents Deadly.

Influenced through playing in Oakland, CA with musicians such as Pharaoh Sanders, The Escovedos, and Carlos Santana, Stev started his first band, Area 39, at age 18, and later, Screens. He produced and engineered Screens' album, Planet Androidia Tour, as well as playing guitar, bass, synth, drums and percussion on all but two tracks.

Screens' CD, Planet Androidia Tour, is availiable for fans of wacky guitar chops and trippy funk metal. To get a copy drop us a line at spindry@wiregraffiti.com.
