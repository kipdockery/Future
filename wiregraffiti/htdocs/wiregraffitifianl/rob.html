A huge Frank Zappa fan, Rob also just shuts up and plays.

Relocating from Toledo, Ohio to join Sci-fi, a band he was contacted by via internet, Rob decided to stay in the Bay Area. Rob joined Wire Graffiti in 2000 and nerve-rackingly his first show was a television spot on Showcase 'Best Of Bay Area Music'. He did great and has been an integral part of the band since, contributing writing ideas and adding to the mayhem. Check out his other project Orbiter 500.
