Rafa Corral, AKA 'Tre Jones', has recently taken over for Tim Sanchez who was our primary bass player from the beginning. We met Rafa when he was playing in a band called Cheshire Jones and was on the bill with Wire Graffiti way back when The Boomerang was still a club on Haight St in SF. He also was a DJ for KCSF and liked our song 'Real World' so he played it and came to shows. We really like Rafa a lot and he has skills with the bass so we asked him to come jam a few years back when we thought we might start to tour. When Tim needed shoulder surgery after we finished this last album it was only natural to get Rafa to come play with us again. Rafa rocks his bass with two other projects, The Plastic Saints and Gaviotas who keep him beating down his strings 24/7.
