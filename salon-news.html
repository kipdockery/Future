We're ready to be inspired by you this year! 2013 is well under way and we already have made some great changes at the shop. We've opened up the front by taking out the window platform which made room for a new blowdry station. We have lots of new color techniques to try, fresh from all the classes we've been hosting and attending with many many advanced classes to come this spring! Come in for our amazing in-salon treatments and take-home deep conditioners that repair over time to help with winter hair blues. Styles are shorter and texture and curl are coming back with a vengence, but smooth and sleek are always in! Come speak to one of our talented team members today and take care of winter ravaged scalp and hair.
