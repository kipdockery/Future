FUTURE Vanity/Novelty: an intimate boutique salon providing future-friendly haircare, makeup, jewelry and accessories.

At FUTURE Vanity/Novelty we believe in the future of your hair, health, and the planet. We are inspired by you, and consider ourselves the catalyst for you to express your own unique style. Your needs are part of our design philosophy.

FUTURE Vanity/Novelty is the vision of owner, Katherine Sawyer, who has created a quieter, healthier, and environmentally friendly way to present a unique hair and fashion experience.

By bringing you the best in sustainable, organic and chemical free haircare, to the repurposed elements featured in the design of the space – we encourage people to take care of themselves, the environment in which they live and work, and everything they love.
